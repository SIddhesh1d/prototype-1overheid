Can I stay more than 90 days in the Netherlands?

Do you want to stay in the Netherlands for more than 90 days, for example for work or study? Or do you want to live with your family in the Netherlands? Then you may need a long-stay visa and / or a residence permit. A long-stay visa is also called an authorization for temporary stay (MVV).

Whether you need an mvv and / or residence permit depends on, among other things, your nationality and your purpose of stay.

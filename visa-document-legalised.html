Why do I need to legalise my document?

You may need to have your foreign documents translated and legalised before you can use them in the Netherlands. Your foreign birth or marriage certificate may be required if you are applying for a residence permit or if you want to register at a municipality in the Netherlands. These are examples of documents that may need legalisation in the country where they are issued. To legalise a document, the competent authorities sign and stamp them. Use our document legalisation check to find out if your documents need translation and legalisation and how you can have your documents legalised.

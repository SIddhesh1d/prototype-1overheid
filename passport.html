Apply or renew passport or ID card

On this page you can start a passport or ID-card application. You can also find out how you can get an emergency passport, and what you must do if your passport is lost or stolen. No matter where you are. Also if you live outside the Netherlands. There are two ways to apply for a passport.

  1. Submit your application digitally via DigiD.
    Select this option if you have a DigiD account and when you have had a passport or ID-card in the last 10 years. The entire application process is done online.
  2. Submit your application in person without DigiD.
    First time passport and ID-card applications must be submitted in person.

Claiming AOW pension if you live outside the Netherlands

This information on this page is also available in:
Polski

If you live outside the Netherlands, how to claim an AOW pension depends on where you live. There are three possibilities.

  1. You live in a country of the (EU)European Union.
    You have a choice. You can submit your AOW pension claim to the pension agency in the country where you live, or in the country where you were last insured.
  2. You live in a country with which the Netherlands has a social security agreement or in a country of the EEA (European Economic Area).
    If you are insured under that country's state pension scheme, you should contact the pension agency in that country.
    If you are not insured under that country's state pension scheme, you should contact the pension agency in the country where you were last insured. If that was the Netherlands, please download and fill out the claim form and get in touch with the Social Insurance Bank (SVB).
  3. If you live in a country not referred to above.
    In that case, please download and fill out the claim form and get in touch with the Social Insurance Bank (SVB).

    You should contact your pension agency at least 6 months before your pension age.

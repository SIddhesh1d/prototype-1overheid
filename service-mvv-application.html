The information on this page is also available in:

You can use this service:

1. If you receive an approval letter for your long-stay visa from the IND, and you have an application number (V-number), you can apply for a long-stay visa that allows you to enter the Netherlands. This permit is also called a MVV-sticker.

2. If you fall under one of the categories below, you can use this service to apply for a residence permit for the Netherlands:

  • a highly skilled migrant seeking employment on an orientational year;
  • an independententrepreneur;
  • a start-up;
  • a foreign investor.
